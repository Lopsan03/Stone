# Quick Start: Escrow Security Features

## 🎯 What's New

Your freelance marketplace now includes **guaranteed escrow payments** with **phase-based release**. This means:

- **For Clients**: Your money is locked & safe until you approve each phase
- **For Freelancers**: Your payment is guaranteed upfront (no payment delays or refusal)

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Switch to Contracts Tab
Click **"Contracts"** in the navigation to see active engagements and the anti-fraud guarantee banner.

### Step 2: Create a Contract
1. Go to **Contracts** tab (must have an accepted relationship first)
2. Click **"Create New Contract"**
3. Define milestones with clear deliverables and amounts
4. Submit - contract will be in "Proposed" status

### Step 3: Fund the Escrow (Clients)
1. Click **"Deposit Escrow"** on the Proposed contract
2. Funds are locked immediately
3. Contract moves to "Active" - work can begin

### Step 4: Complete & Approve Milestones
**Freelancer action:**
- Mark each phase "Mark Done" when complete

**Client action:**
- Review work in "Awaiting Review" status
- Click "Approve & Release" to release payment

---

## 🔒 Security Features Explained

### Locked Funds
- See the amber progress bar showing **locked amount**
- Neither party can access this money
- Only releases when milestone is approved

### Sequential Phases
- Can't skip to Phase 3 until Phase 1 & 2 are paid
- Prevents scope creep and ensures accountability
- Provides clear deliverables for each phase

### Dispute Resolution
- Either party can flag a milestone as disputed
- Platform arbitrators review the case
- Funds held safely in escrow during review
- Decision: Refund or Release

## 📋 Milestone Management

### For Each Milestone:
```
┌─────────────────────┐
│ Pending (Locked)    │  ← Freelancer works on phase
│ Funds: Locked       │
│ [Mark Done]         │
└──────────┬──────────┘
           │
┌──────────▼──────────┐
│ Completed (Review)  │  ← Client reviews work
│ Funds: Locked       │
│ [Approve & Release] │
└──────────┬──────────┘
           │
┌──────────▼──────────┐
│ Paid (Complete)     │  ← Funds released
│ Funds: Released ✅  │
└─────────────────────┘
```

## 📞 Need Help?

### Understanding Locked Funds
- 🔒 = Money in escrow, not with either party
- Released when: Freelancer marks done + Client approves
- Safe during disputes: Held until arbitrators decide

### Initiating a Dispute
1. Client: Click "Flag for Review" if work quality is unsatisfactory
2. Describe the issue clearly
3. Platform will review evidence
4. Decision: Refund or Release within 3-5 days

### Checking Contract Status
- **Proposed**: Awaiting client to deposit funds
- **Active**: Funds locked, work in progress
- **Completed**: All milestones paid
- **Disputed**: Under review by arbitrators

- ✓ Establish communication channels

## 🎓 Understanding the Guarantee

**The Promise**: 
> Your locked funds are safe. Freelancers can't take your money without delivering. Clients can't refuse to pay for completed work.

**How It Works**:
1. **Deposit** → Client funds locked immediately
2. **Work** → Freelancer completes phase
3. **Verify** → Client reviews deliverables
4. **Release** → Payment only moves when both agree
5. **Repeat** → Process continues for each phase

**What Makes It Safe**:
- 🔒 Funds locked on-chain (neither party controls)
- 📋 Sequential phases (can't skip forward)
- 👥 Dual approval (both must confirm)
- ⚖️ Arbitration (disputes resolved fairly)
